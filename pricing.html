Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the API pricing work? ▼
Our pricing is based on a monthly subscription that includes a set quota of API calls. If you exceed this quota on the Professional plan, you'll be billed at a rate of $0.001 per additional call. Enterprise plans have unlimited calls subject to fair use.
Can I change plans at any time? ▼
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your billing dashboard. Upgrades are prorated and effective immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial available? ▼
Absolutely. We offer a 14-day free trial on our Professional plan. No credit card is required to sign up and start testing our models.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual Enterprise billing.
What happens to my data? Is it used for training? ▼
We take data privacy very seriously. Your API requests and data are NEVER used to train our foundational models. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and you own all rights to your inputs and outputs.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits or startups? ▼
Yes! We have a special program for early-stage startups, educational institutions, and 501(c)(3) non-profits. Please contact our sales team with details about your organization to apply.
