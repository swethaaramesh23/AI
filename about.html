Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a team of leading AI researchers from top universities, STACKLY AI began with a simple yet ambitious goal: to make enterprise-grade artificial intelligence accessible to businesses of all sizes.

What started as a small consulting firm has grown into a global technology provider, powering over 50 million API calls daily. We've spent the last several years refining our proprietary models and building a scalable infrastructure that can handle the most demanding computational workloads.

Today, we stand at the forefront of the AI revolution, partnering with industry leaders to solve complex problems through machine learning, computer vision, and generative AI.

🎯

Our Mission

To democratize access to advanced artificial intelligence, enabling organizations to unlock new efficiencies, discover hidden insights, and create unprecedented value for their customers through ethical and transparent AI systems.

Our Vision

We envision a future where human ingenuity is seamlessly augmented by intelligent automation, where complex global challenges are solved collaboratively by humans and AI, creating a more efficient, sustainable, and prosperous world.
